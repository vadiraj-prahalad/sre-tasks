# Kannada AI Platform – Knowledge Article Model v2

Last Updated: 2026-07-08

## Goal

The knowledge model defines how verified Kannada knowledge is stored, reviewed, refreshed, embedded, searched, and served to users.

This model should support:

- Human-written knowledge
- Admin CMS articles
- Bulk imported knowledge packs
- Internet-imported draft evidence
- Aliases
- Related topics
- Future mobile and web beta
- Future reviewer workflow

---

## Core Principle

The platform should never treat raw internet content or AI-generated text as trusted knowledge.

Trusted knowledge must be:

```text
Collected
↓
Reviewed
↓
Approved
↓
Published
↓
Refreshed into searchable knowledge
